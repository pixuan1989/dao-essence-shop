Life energy pattern analysis is an important part of traditional Chinese wisdom study. It uses the year, month, day, and hour of one's birth, corresponding to the Heavenly Stems and Earthly Branches, to form energy patterns. This provides insights into one's life trajectory, personality traits, and life patterns.
Basic information includes: name, gender, birth date (solar or lunar), and birth hour. The more accurate the birth hour, the more precise the analysis. If you're unsure of the exact hour, you can provide an approximate time range, and the analyst will infer based on other information.
