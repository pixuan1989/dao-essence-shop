🔒 We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy for standard items. Your satisfaction is our priority.

You may return items within 30 days of delivery if they meet the following conditions:

Due to the spiritual and personalized nature of our products, the following items cannot be returned or refunded:

How to Initiate a Return — Step by Step

Returns are simple. Follow the steps below and we'll take care of the rest:

1

Email orders@daoessence.com or message us on WhatsApp (+86 181 1575 5283) with the following:
· Your Order ID (found in your confirmation email)

· 1–3 photos showing the item's condition

2

We will review your request and respond within 48 business hours. If approved, we will send you:
· Return Merchandise Authorisation (RMA) number

Ship the Item Back

Pack the item securely in its original box (if possible) and ship to the address provided. Please note:
· Write your RMA number clearly on the outside of the package
· Use a trackable shipping service and retain your tracking number
· Return shipping costs are borne by the customer, except in cases of our error (wrong or damaged item)

4

Refund Issued After Inspection

Once we receive and inspect the returned item (usually within 2 business days of arrival), your refund will be processed automatically. You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is issued.
