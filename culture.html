Space Energy

Space Energy Optimization

Home Energy Flow Entrance bright, bedroom peaceful. A well-arranged home promotes harmony and positive energy. Keep the entrance uncluttered and the bedroom a sanctuary of rest and tranquility.
Abundance Area Diagonal from entrance. The southeast corner is traditionally associated with abundance. Place positive energy items here to support financial well-being and prosperity.
Plants Money tree, lucky bamboo. Living plants bring vital life energy and symbolize growth and prosperity. Choose plants with round leaves and avoid thorny varieties indoors.
Water Flow Flow inward for positive energy. Water symbolizes abundance in traditional space energy practices. Ensure water features flow towards the center of the home to gather positive energy and abundance.
Negative Energy Screens, mirrors, crystals. Use protective elements to deflect harmful energy. Mirrors can redirect energy, while crystals absorb negative vibrations and create a harmonious atmosphere.
Relationship Area Southwest position. The southwest corner governs relationships. Decorate with pairs of objects, romantic imagery, and warm colors to support relationship harmony.
Bedroom Headboard against wall. Position the bed with a solid headboard against a solid wall for support and stability. Avoid placing the bed directly under a beam or facing the door.
Door Position Avoid door facing door. Conflicting doors create disruptive energy flow. Use screens, curtains, or plants to soften the energy and maintain a peaceful transition between spaces.
