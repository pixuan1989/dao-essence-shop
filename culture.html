Feng Shui

Feng Shui Encyclopedia

Feng Shui is ancient wisdom studying the relationship between environment and people. By adjusting the energy flow of living spaces, it achieves harmony between heaven and humanity, attracting good fortune and avoiding misfortune.

🏠 Home Feng Shui

  • Entrance: Should be bright and tidy, place plants to welcome good luck
  • Living Room: Sofa should back solid wall, round coffee table preferred
  • Bedroom: Headboard shouldn't face door, mirror shouldn't face bed
  • Kitchen: Stove shouldn't face door or sink, avoid water-fire conflict

💰 Wealth Feng Shui

  • Wealth Position: Diagonal from entrance, place treasure bowl or Pixiu
  • Water Flow: Aquarium water flows inward, symbolizing wealth entering
  • Plants: Money tree and lucky bamboo enhance wealth energy
  • Lighting: Keep wealth position bright, symbolizing prosperous fortune

⚡ Negative Energy Solutions

  • Door Facing Door: Use screen or curtain to block, or hang five-emperor coins
  • Beam Overhead: Install false ceiling or place gourd to resolve
  • Sharp Corners: Use mirror reflection or plant blocking
  • Missing Corner: Use Mount Tai stone or zodiac ornaments for remedy

💑 Love Feng Shui

  • Peach Blossom Position: Find based on zodiac, place flowers or crystals
  • Bedroom Layout: Headboard against solid wall, avoid beam overhead
  • Paired Items: Place paired objects symbolizing harmonious relationships
  • Pink Tones: Use pink appropriately in bedroom to enhance love luck
