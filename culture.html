Feng Shui

Feng Shui Wisdom

Home Feng Shui Entrance bright, bedroom peaceful. A well-arranged home promotes harmony and prosperity. Keep the entrance uncluttered and the bedroom a sanctuary of rest and tranquility.
Wealth Position Diagonal from entrance. The southeast corner is traditionally associated with wealth accumulation. Place auspicious items here to enhance financial fortune and attract abundance.
Plants Money tree, lucky bamboo. Living plants bring vital Qi energy and symbolize growth and prosperity. Choose plants with round leaves and avoid thorny varieties indoors.
Water Flow Flow inward for wealth. Water symbolizes wealth and prosperity in Feng Shui. Ensure water features flow towards the center of the home to gather Qi and abundance.
Negative Energy Screens, mirrors, crystals. Use protective elements to deflect harmful Qi. Mirrors can redirect energy, while crystals absorb negative vibrations and create a harmonious atmosphere.
Love Luck Peach blossom position. The southwest corner governs relationships. Decorate with pairs of objects, romantic imagery, and warm colors to enhance romantic fortune.
Bedroom Headboard against wall. Position the bed with a solid headboard against a solid wall for support and stability. Avoid placing the bed directly under a beam or facing the door.
Door Position Avoid door facing door. Conflicting doors create hostile Qi flow. Use screens, curtains, or plants to soften the energy and maintain a peaceful transition between spaces.
