**1. The Toilet: Your "Wealth Vault"**

Treat your toilet as a symbolic vault of wealth. Keep it spotless—every corner matters. Just as you wouldn't leave money lying around in the dirt, ensure this area is free of grime to prevent "leaking" your financial energy.



**2. The Fridge: Your "Energetic Bank Account"**

Your refrigerator mirrors your financial health. It shouldn't be empty (scarcity) or overstuffed (stagnation). Practice regular turnover: remove expired items and keep it organized. Fresh food equals fresh cash flow.



**3. The Entryway: The "Gateway to Luck"**

The foyer is where opportunity enters. Place vibrant, thriving flowers here. If the flowers wilt, replace them immediately—dying plants symbolize fading luck. Keep this space uncluttered to allow energy to flow freely.



**4. The Wealth Corner: Light & Life**

Identify your home's wealth area (typically the farthest left corner from the front door). Place a **"Long-Life Lamp"**​ (a lamp that stays on low light) and lush, upright plants here. Choose plants with large, round leaves (like Jade or Money Trees). Avoid vines or spiky plants.



**5. The Wardrobe: Curating Your Confidence**

Clothes hold energy. Declutter your closet ruthlessly. Only keep items that make you feel powerful and confident. Discard or donate damaged, ill-fitting, or "unloved" clothes—they drag down your personal vibration.



**6. Air Quality: The Foundation of Health**

Invest in a dehumidifier and air purifier. In holistic health, illness often stems from accumulated dampness and stale energy. Clean air supports better sleep, higher energy levels, and mental clarity.



**7. Bedding: Inviting Romance**

If seeking a partner, avoid checkered or grid-patterned bedding. In energy work, grids create "blocks." Opt for solid colors or gentle patterns to invite relationship energy.



**8. Mirrors: Reflection & Perception**

Choose full-length mirrors with clean, square frames. Avoid distorted or irregular shapes, as they are believed to attract chaotic or "shadow" energy. Square mirrors promote stability and clarity.



**9. The Nightstand: The "Energy Triangle"**

Your bedside is a power source. Place a pair of matching warm-toned lamps on either side to represent partnership and balance. Add a pleasant scent (diffuser or natural incense) to activate the senses. *Note: While some traditions suggest fresh flowers, if you are in a relationship, opt for lamps and scent to avoid potential conflict.*



**10. Furniture: Stability & Grounding**

Choose sofas and dining tables made of solid wood. Wood carries stable, grounding energy. Avoid furniture with sharp edges or flimsy metal frames, as they can create restless energy in the home.
